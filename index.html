TECHNOLOGY

How xplyweb works

01
Rust cryptographic primitives are compiled to WebAssembly using wasm-pack.
02
The frontend (React + Vite) loads the WASM module and exposes clean interactive interfaces.
03
Every proof runs locally in your browser — no servers, no data leaves your device.
